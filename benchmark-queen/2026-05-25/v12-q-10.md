## Headline takeaways

1. **10-queue overhead is similarly free in 0.12.19**: comparing `v12-bp-10` (1 queue, 31.8 k msg/s) vs `v12-q-10` (10 queues, 31.6 k msg/s) — only −0.6 % difference. 2. **Same 22 % version gap** as `v12-bp-10`: v12 sustains 31.6 k msg/s vs 0.14's 40.5 k. The version delta is about the same with 1 queue or 10 queues — multi-queue is *not* the variable.
